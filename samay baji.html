Samay baji

Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup beaten rice (chiura)
  2. 1/2 cup boiled black-eyed peas
  3. 1/2 cup boiled black soybeans
  4. 1/2 cup boiled black lentils
  5. 1/2 cup boiled diced potatoes
  6. 1/2 cup boiled sweet potatoes
  7. 1/2 cup boiled barley grains
  8. 1/2 cup boiled green peas
  9. 1/2 cup boiled diced carrots
  10. 1/2 cup boiled diced cucumber
  11. 1/2 cup boiled diced pumpkin
  12. 1/2 cup yogurt
  13. 2 tablespoons mustard oil
  14. 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  15. 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
  16. 1 tablespoon chopped ginger
  17. 1 tablespoon chopped green chilies
  18. 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  19. 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  20. Salt to taste

Procedures:

  1. Arrange the beaten rice and boiled ingredients on a plate or in separate bowls.
  2. In a small bowl, mix yogurt, mustard oil, sesame seeds, cilantro, ginger, green chilies, cumin powder, coriander powder, and salt to make a spicy yogurt sauce.
  3. Serve the beaten rice and boiled ingredients with the spicy yogurt sauce. Enjoy the flavors and textures of Samay Baji!
